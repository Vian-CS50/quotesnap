# Age Workaround — ABN & Stripe Setup (Do Tonight)

You are 16. In Australia you CAN:
- Hold an ABN as a sole trader with guardian consent/co-signing (no statutory minimum age)
- Be the technical operator while a parent is the legal owner on paper for Stripe
- Sign commercial contracts with guardian involvement

You CAN:
- Build and sell software through a parent's legal entity
- Be the technical operator while a parent is the legal owner on paper
- Receive money transferred from a parent's account

This is completely legal, normal, and how many under-18 founders operate.

CORRECTION: The previous version of this file incorrectly stated you cannot hold an ABN under 18. You CAN apply for an ABN at any age — under 18 requires a parent/guardian to co-sign or be present during the application at abr.gov.au.

---

## Step 1: Parent Applies for ABN

1. Go to https://abr.gov.au
2. Click "Apply for an ABN"
3. Select "Sole trader"
4. Business name: "QuoteSnap Software Services" (or just use their name)
5. Business activity: "Software publishing" or "Computer system design"
6. Takes 15 minutes. ABN issued immediately.

---

## Step 2: Parent Opens Stripe Account

1. Go to https://stripe.com/au
2. Sign up with parent's details (must match ABN holder)
3. Connect parent's bank account for payouts
4. Set business name to "QuoteSnap Software Services"
5. Use the ABN from Step 1
6. In Stripe settings, set default currency to AUD

---

## Step 3: Update Your Code

1. In `.env` file, add parent's Stripe secret key:
   STRIPE_SECRET_KEY=sk_live_...
   STRIPE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY=pk_live_...

2. In Stripe dashboard, create one product:
   - Name: "QuoteSnap Pro Monthly"
   - Price: $29 AUD / month
   - No free trial (you handle trial manually via messaging)

3. The checkout API in main.py already calls `/api/create-checkout-session`.
   Ensure it creates a subscription, not a one-time payment.

---

## Step 4: Terms of Service

Add a simple Terms of Service page to your website. It should say:

"By subscribing to QuoteSnap, you agree to pay $29 AUD per month (or $19 AUD per month for founder pricing). You may cancel anytime. All payments are processed securely by Stripe. QuoteSnap Software Services is not liable for inaccuracies in AI-generated quotes. Always review quotes before sending to clients."

This is standard SaaS click-to-accept. No signatures. No negotiation. No age check.

---

## Step 5: Money Flow

1. Customer pays through Stripe → lands in parent's bank account
2. Parent transfers money to you (or you use their card for API credits)
3. Keep a simple spreadsheet of revenue vs costs (Moonshot API, Stripe fees)
4. At tax time, parent declares the income on their tax return
   - Deduct API costs, hosting, domain as business expenses
   - If you pay yourself, that is a contractor payment (also deductible)

---

## What to Tell Customers

Nothing. Customers do not care who holds the ABN. They care that the product works and their credit card payment is secure.

On your website, you are "The QuoteSnap Team." No photos of you. No age mentioned. No founder story.

---

## What NOT To Do

- Do NOT lie about your age to Stripe. They will verify identity and ban the account.
- Do NOT use a friend's or relative's ABN without their explicit knowledge and consent.
- Do NOT skip the Terms of Service. It protects you from disputes.
